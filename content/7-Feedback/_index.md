After participating in the **AWS First Cloud AI Journey** internship program at **Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam**, I found it to be a valuable learning experience that connected academic knowledge with the practical process of delivering a technology project.

### Program Feedback

The program was well aligned with the field of Cloud Computing and encouraged interns to independently research and work with AWS services. The guidance sessions, reference materials, and progress reports helped me develop a more structured working method, break down complex problems, and track results through each project stage.

Through the project **End-to-End Test Automation with Playwright and Docker on AWS**, I applied knowledge of AWS, Docker, Playwright, Python/Boto3, and event-driven architecture. Team collaboration also improved my communication, coordination, and accountability for assigned work.

### Mentorship Feedback

The mentors and program coordinators provided technology direction, architecture feedback, and support when challenges arose during implementation. Their comments helped the team identify weaknesses, refine the solution, and produce a clearer and more feasible result.

I particularly appreciated the approach of encouraging interns to investigate problems before asking for assistance. It strengthened my independent thinking, documentation research, and ability to explain technical issues clearly when seeking support.

### Suggestions

To make future internship cohorts even more effective, I would like to suggest the following:

- Organize additional short sessions on system architecture, security, cost optimization, and real-world AWS deployment practices.
- Provide detailed evaluation criteria and project milestones at the beginning of the program so interns can plan and monitor their progress more effectively.
- Allocate more time for architecture and code reviews at key stages of each project.
- Create more opportunities for teams to present their solutions, discuss challenges, and learn from one another.
- Maintain a shared collection of learning materials, hands-on labs, and sample projects for interns to explore after the program.

### Acknowledgement

I sincerely thank **Amazon Web Services Vietnam**, the **AWS First Cloud AI Journey** program, the mentors, and my team members for their guidance and support throughout the internship. The knowledge, experience, and feedback I received provide an important foundation for improving my technical skills, professional working style, and career direction in cloud computing.
